About me
Kushaank Chawla — Motion Designer

Motion Graphics Designer,
Artist & Entrepreneur

As a Motion Graphics Designer, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with leading brands across technology, culture and lifestyle.

Renowned for my innovative approach to creativity, I began my career with small youtuber, swiftly establishing my distinctive motion design style. I am drawn to the details — the easing of an animation, the weight of a transition, the moment when motion and meaning click into place.

Currently, I work with brands through contract-based projects, consistently pushing the boundaries of digital design and creative strategy.
